More About Melvin

Background:

We decided to foster Melvin in the summer of 2023. He got his eyes removed after a month or so of fostering. We got to spend a few more weeks with him, and then he was diagnosed with Feline Leukemia Virus. By the time we had found out, our other cats had already been infected. We decided to rescue him, and it was the best decision ever.


Personality:

Melvin has as many personalities as he has names. Melvin, Melly, Mister, Buster, Smelvin, Smelly Melly, Mister Smelvin, Junior, and Tiny. He's very smart and aware of his surroundings. He's energetic and loving.
